## Program Details

The MotorPH Payroll System is a Java-based program that automates payroll processing. 

- Users log in as either **Employee** or **Payroll Staff**.
- **Employees** can view their personal information such as employee number, name, and birthday.
- **Payroll Staff** can process payroll for one or all employees.
- The system reads data from CSV files for employee information and attendance.
- It calculates total hours worked, gross salary, government deductions (SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG), and net salary.
- Payroll is divided into **two cut-off periods** per month (1–15 and 16–end of month). Tax and Deductions are applied at the 2nd cut-off period.
